We believe in sharing our customer feedback both so we can improve our services and so you can hear how others have found our work.

"It's truly hard to overstate what an amazing job Tiles 4 You did for us. My family has been trying to tile our own bathroom for over 20 years now, but we have failed at every attempt we've made. Finally, we threw in the towel and had Tiles 4 You lend us a hand. Best decision we've made in a while, and now we finally have a usable bathroom. Thanks Tiles 4 You!"

“I hired Tiles 4 You to redo my bathroom, and honestly, they did a great job, but… their tile installer insists on singing ‘Eye of the Tiger’ at full volume while working. I have to admit, the tiles were laid with the precision of a champion fighter entering the ring, but the serenades were unexpected. My bathroom floor looks flawless—like it could take on Apollo Creed in a boxing match. Just be prepared for a concert if you hire them.”

“Tiles 4 You showed up at 6 a.m. on a Saturday with what I can only describe as a portable fog machine and mood lighting. The installer said it helped him ‘connect spiritually with the tiles.’ By noon, I had a kitchen backsplash made entirely out of those little hexagonal mosaic tiles… arranged to look like the Mona Lisa! Impressive, but I just wanted plain white tiles. When I pointed this out, he gave me a long lecture about ‘the transcendent nature of grout’ before disappearing, leaving the fog machine behind. Would not recommend if you’re not into avant-garde tile art.”

“I was excited to get new tiles in my living room, but the Tiles 4 You crew arrived dressed in full medieval armor. They said it was a tradition to ‘fight the old floor demons before laying the new tiles.’ After a 20-minute sword fight with the existing flooring (which was, frankly, thrilling to watch), they did a solid job. The tiles are straight, the grout lines are crisp, but I’m still finding bits of chainmail in the corners. Three stars for the show, but next time, I’ll pass on the jousting.”
